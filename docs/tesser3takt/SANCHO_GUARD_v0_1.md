## 0. Scope

This file defines the Sancho-Guard as a fall-tolerant grounding module for tesser3TAKT.

It uses Don 6, Sancho 5, and Sanchos Rueckenpanzer as literary-metaphorical role-forms. These motifs are used as [METAPHOR] and [MODEL] roles, not as proof claims.

It references geometric quantization and metaplectic correction only as source-bound decorative/model roles already constrained by SOURCE_DECORATION_MAP_v0_1.md.

It does not claim that Don Quijote, Sancho Panza, geometric quantization, or metaplectic correction prove tesser3TAKT.

This file remains ANNEX documentation only.

## 1. Core Definition

[MODEL] Sancho-Guard is the asymmetrical grounding and damping layer of tesser3TAKT.

Don 6 = ideal projection, overheight, continuous possibility, giant-reading.

Sancho 5 = embodied grounding, windmill-reading, reality-friction.

Sanchos Rueckenpanzer = fall-tolerant damping wedge between ideal overheight and embodied reality.

Sancho macht das Ideal nicht kleiner; er macht es landefaehig.

Guard: Do not frame Sancho as anti-ideal or anti-imagination. Sancho protects the ideal by making it landable.

[METAPHOR] Don 6 names the upward vector of imagination.

Don 6 may mark:

## 4. Sanchos Rueckenpanzer

[METAPHOR] / [MODEL] Sanchos Rueckenpanzer names the protective asymmetry between ideal overheight and embodied reality.

Sanchos Rueckenpanzer may mark:

1. asymmetrical damping wedge
2. protective backplate
3. 5-over-6 Schraegstellung
4. fall-tolerant buffer
5. reentry stabilizer

The Rueckenpanzer keeps Don 6 and Sancho 5 in productive non-identity. It absorbs ideal impact without erasing the ideal.

Sanchos Rueckenpanzer ist der asymmetrische Daempfungskeil zwischen Riese und Muehle.

Guard: Do not frame the Rueckenpanzer as unbreakable, audit-proof, or triumphant. Frame it as fall-tolerant, guarded, and reentry-capable.

[MODEL] 5-over-6 beat is the living Schraegstellung between ideal projection and embodied grounding.

It is not a perfect symmetry.

It is an intentionally uneven rhythm that prevents sterile 6-over-6 collapse.

The 5-over-6 beat keeps the transition alive by refusing total symmetry.

Guard: Do not turn 5-over-6 into numerological proof. It is a rhythm-role.

## 9. Fall-Tolerance

[MODEL] Fall-tolerance means the system can absorb failed or excessive projection without turning it into shame, proof, or collapse.

A fall-tolerant guard allows correction without humiliating imagination.

The Sancho-Guard protects both sides:

1. It protects Sancho from being flattened by ideal overheight.
2. It protects Don from being killed by premature realism.

Falltauglich ist staerker als unzerstoerbar.

Guard: Do not use fall-tolerance as an excuse for sloppy claims. A fall still requires guard, reentry, and possible STOP.
