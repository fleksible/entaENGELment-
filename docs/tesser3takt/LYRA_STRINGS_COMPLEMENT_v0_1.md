## 2. Lyra as Resonance Body for POVs

[MODEL] Lyra means resonance body for perspectives.

The Lyra does not unify all perspectives into sameness. It makes their differences tunable. A tuned relation may be stable, tense, disharmonic, or overtone-rich without becoming an identity claim.

Pauli / Slater may be referenced only as a source-bound model role. In this layer, the exclusion-role reading means that no two POVs should collapse into the same functional occupation when difference is required.

Guard: Do not claim physical Pauli/Slater equivalence. Use it only as an exclusion-role metaphor/model.

## 6. Disharmony

[MODEL] Disharmony is not simply error. Disharmony can be the audible edge of transition. It may mark POV-shift, incomplete integration, emerging overtone, or old tuning becoming insufficient.

Canonical sentence:

"Disharmonie ist der hoerbare Rand einer noch nicht integrierten Kontextur."

Guard: Disharmony is not automatically valuable. It must still pass guard and reentry checks.

## 7. Overtones

[MODEL] Overtone means latent future, possibility halo, or additional resonance layer.

Ground tone means manifest statement or current POV. Overtone means implied connection, future possibility, or non-dominant resonance.

Overtones keep a form from becoming too final too early. They allow additional resonance to remain audible without promoting it into a proof claim.

Guard: Do not turn ambiguity into proof. An overtone is a possible resonance, not a claim.

## 8. Frozen Waves and String State

[MODEL] A string at rest is stored possibility. A strike actualizes it. Vibration is event. Overtone is surplus resonance. Decay returns the event into sediment.

Sequence:

resting string -> strike -> vibration -> overtone -> decay -> sediment

This sequence connects to V+ / enriched past as stored resonance history. What has sounded can return as sediment, weight, and future tuning condition.

[MODEL] Finger means tactile membrane operator. Pressure on the string is a Piezo-like reading role. Too little pressure gives no clear tone. Too much pressure chokes the string. Right pressure lets the string speak.

Fuehligkeit means pre-verbal phase sensitivity: the ability to sense transition quality before the explicit concept is fully available.

Required sentence:

"Fuehligkeit ist der Finger auf der Saite, bevor der Ton weiss, wie er heissen wird."

Guard: Do not treat Fuehligkeit as evidence. It is a sensitivity role that still requires guard and reentry.
